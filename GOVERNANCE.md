# Governance

This repository is part of the cloud-itonami fleet and follows its governance model.

## Blueprint Governance

At blueprint maturity (current state):
- Design review and specification updates are community-driven.
- The independent `:tobacco-manufacturing-governor` is the compliance authority for all proposals when the actor is implemented.
- No proposals bypass the Governor.

## Implementation Governance (Phase 2+)

When implementation begins (Wave 3):
- All code contributions require review and test coverage.
- The audit ledger is append-only and immutable.
- Escalations to human operators are never bypassed.
- Marketing and health-claim authority remain exclusively human/compliance-officer domain.

## Regulatory Compliance

Tobacco manufacturing is a regulated industry. This blueprint is designed to support compliance, not circumvent it:
- Excise-tax tracking is transparent and auditable.
- Health warning compliance always escalates to licensed human review.
- All processing-line control remains under licensed plant operator authority.

This blueprint CANNOT be used for unregulated or non-compliant operations.
